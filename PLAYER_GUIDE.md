## ğŸ¯ Gameplay Tips

### Combat
1. **Aim Carefully**: Enemies take damage from bullets - aim for them!
2. **Use Cover**: Hide behind platforms to avoid enemy bullets
3. **Manage Ammo**: Reload when safe, collect ammo pickups
4. **Weapon Switching**: Different weapons work better in different situations
   - Shotgun: Close range, multiple enemies
   - Laser: Fast enemies, long range
   - Rocket: High damage, area effect
   - Machine Gun: Rapid fire, many enemies
   - Sniper: High damage, single targets

### Platforming
1. **Jump Timing**: Time your jumps carefully to avoid spikes and lava
2. **Moving Platforms**: Wait for platforms to come to you
3. **Checkpoints**: Activate checkpoints to respawn there if you die
4. **Exploration**: Look for hidden coins and power-ups

### Power-ups
- **Health Pickup** (Red Cross): Restores health
- **Ammo Pickup** (Yellow Box): Restores ammo
- **Shield Pickup** (Blue Shield): Temporary invincibility
- **Speed Boost** (Green Arrow): Temporary speed increase
- **Damage Boost** (Red Star): Temporary damage multiplier

### Collectibles
- **Coins**: Collect coins to spend in the shop
- **Keys**: Collect keys (may unlock doors in future updates)
